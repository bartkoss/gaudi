Antoni Gaudí

A timeline of the life of Antoni Gaudí

Timeline

  • 1852: born in Reus, near Barcelona
  • 1869: started studying architecture in Barcelona
  • 1878: finished studying architecture
  • 1882: Gaudi got the assignment to continue the work on the Sagrada Familia
  • 1900: he got the assignment to build Parc Güell
  • 1926: after a serious accident with a tram, he passed away on the 10th of july
